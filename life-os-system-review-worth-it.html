Weekly Actions ? Daily Tasks. Every task on your daily list is traceable back to a quarterly objective, which connects to your annual vision. This eliminates the "busy but not productive" trap.

If you buy the Life OS System for only one component, make it the weekly review workflow. Users report this single habit reduces overwhelm by 60%+ within the first two weeks.

Pro Tip: Schedule your weekly review for Sunday evening (30 minutes) or Monday morning. Consistency matters more than timing. After four weeks, it becomes a non-negotiable ritual.
