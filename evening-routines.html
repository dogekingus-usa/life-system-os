The Tomorrow Prep Ritual

Five to ten minutes of preparation saves 30+ minutes of morning decision fatigue. Lay out your clothes. Pack your bag. Review your calendar. Write down your top three priorities for tomorrow. Set your coffee maker timer. When you wake up to a prepared day, you start executing instead of planning.

Gratitude and Reflection

Before sleep, write down three things you're grateful for from the day. This simple practice reduces anxiety, improves sleep quality, and shifts your brain toward positive pattern recognition. It takes 60 seconds and compounds powerfully over time.

What to Avoid

  • Alcohol within 3 hours of bed (disrupts REM sleep)
  • Caffeine after 2 PM (half-life is 5-6 hours)
  • Heavy meals within 2 hours of bed
  • Intense exercise within 1 hour of bed
  • Work emails or stressful conversations after dinner
